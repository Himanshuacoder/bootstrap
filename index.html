    Wild Life

    Your Donation to the WildLife conservation organisations will help in,

    1. Saving the animals from the poachers
    These organisations convinces poachers to turn in their spears for shovels by teaching them beekeeping, Tilapia fish farming and how to grow vegetables.
    2. Rescuing and rehabilitisation
    A team of dedicated volunteers rescue several number of animals and nurture them back to health and then release them back into the wild.
    3. Reducing the Human-Wildlife conflicts
    We are successfully keeping animals out of farmer's fields by using things like beehive fences. Animals like elephants invade Farmer's crop fields destroying everything. Angry farmers spear them. At times people get hurt. Your donation helps in avoiding such situations.
    4.Protecting the animals specially focusing on endangered ones
    This simply helps in conserving the cycle of nature as we equally depend on them.

    Slum Areas

    Your Donation will help us in providing them,

    1. 3 Meals a Day
    We use the donation money to help in providing food to the people living in te slums. This helps them to be healthier and maintain proper hygiene and immunity to diseases.
    2. Proper House
    These organisations try to build these people a livable space with proper hygiene inside and out.
    3. Building Better Community
    A small team of dedicated volunteers teach and educate the people about different fields to help them settle in various professions.
    4. Children's Education
    Your donation goes to the education of the children living in these areas. This inturn will help these children and their communities to get out of the slums in the future.
